INX Technologies - Foundation Document (2025)

Prepared by: Shreyash Sinha (Founder)
Project Start: After 12th Board Exams

1. Vision Statement

INX Technologies aims to build INX.OS - India's Own Operating System not based on AOSP it will be fully independent, created for speed, privacy, independence, and long-term national innovation. Over the next few decades, INX will expand from software into hardware, and eventually into electric vehicles and mobility ecosystems.

2. Purpose of This Document

This document is the official starting point of INX.

It defines:

  • What INX will become
  • What the first version (INX Alpha) must achieve
  • What I need to learn before starting
  • Roadmap for the next 10 years
  • Rules to avoid failure
  • Identity and mission clarity

This document will be updated as INX evolves.

3. Mission of INX

Build a fully Indian technology ecosystem that combines:

  • A modern, high-performance operating system
  • A secure and private mobile experience
  • Future INX devices (phones, wearables, EVs)
  • Homegrown technology that reduces dependence on foreign systems

6. Failure Resistance Strategy

To prevent early collapse

Avoid

  • Taking shortcuts
  • Building everything alone
  • Depending on one income source
  • Trying to beat Android on day one
  • Over-hiring without money
  • Ignoring long-term execution

Do

  • Build prototype first
  • Create audience early
  • Learn consistently
  • Keep costs near zero
  • Build automations for stable income
  • Create partnerships, not competition
  • Build INX step by step
  • Maintain founder discipline

7. Early Risks to Watch

  • Burnout
  • No income ---> no sustainability
  • No team ---> no real OS
  • Wrong expectations ---> frustration
  • Spending early ---> running out of money
  • Poor technical foundation ---> multi-year delay

8. Why India Needs INX

  • Rising need for data privacy
  • Independency from any foreign Operating System (Android or iOS)
  • National security
  • Economic independence
  • Growing digital population
  • Government interest in indigenous tech
  • Large Indian brands may want a homegrown alternative

Note: BharOS is enterprise-focused, not consumer-focused the public market is open.

9. Why Indian Companies May Support INX

Strong reason: Indian OEMs may seek alternatives to reduce reliance on Google licensing and get more customization freedom. INX offers:

  • A low-cost, Indian alternative
  • Privacy-focused marketing angle
  • Faster update cycle
  • Full customization freedom
